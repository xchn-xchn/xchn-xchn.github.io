Xchn is a presentation of selected electronic music works originally composed in stereo, reinterpreted through a multichannel speaker setup spread throughout the hollows and mezzanines of the Mimerlaven mining facility. For an extended day session, visitors at the 2016 edition of Norbergfestival were given the opportunity to experience and explore a continuous stream of electronic sonorities across the mine hall; Analog synthesizer drones, computer music, string ensembles, esoteric percussion. A curational spectrum encompassing a generous width and breadth of the most exquisite spectral transformations imaginable ranging from the early days of electronic music until today.
